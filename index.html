How Each Study Works
Proposal 1 · AI Triage Support
1
Retrospective chart pull — de-identified triage notes from existing ED records; no new patient contact
2
LLM triage assignment — feed chief complaint + vitals to an LLM; record the AI-assigned ESI level
3
Accuracy comparison — compare AI ESI to nurse-assigned ESI and to final disposition as ground truth
4
Equity analysis — stratify accuracy by age, sex, language, and insurance to assess differential performance
5
Statistical analysis — weighted kappa, sensitivity/specificity, subgroup comparisons in R or SPSS
6
Write & submit — frame as AI safety + equity; target AI in Medicine or JAMIA
Proposal 2 · Discharge Comprehension
1
Recruit at discharge — enroll consenting adult patients being discharged from Brookdale ED
2
Baseline comprehension — teach-back immediately post-discharge instruction; score retention of 5 key items
3
Intervention arm — randomize to standard instructions vs. plain-language or illustrated discharge sheet
4
Follow-up call — 48–72 hr phone survey to assess recall, adherence, and return visit rate
5
Statistical analysis — comprehension scores, readmission rates, subgroup analysis by health literacy level
6
Write & submit — frame as patient safety + QI; target Annals of EM or Academic EM
